# Design Details

## Slicers
1. **Product Category**: Enables filtering by product categories.
2. **Payment Method**: Filters based on payment methods.

## Charts
1. **Stacked Column Chart**:
   - **Y-Axis**: Order Total
   - **X-Axis**: Product Size
   - **Legend**: Product Category
   - **Title**: Order Total by Product Size and Product Category for 2023

2. **Donut Chart**:
   - **Values**: Order Total Share
   - **Legend**: Product Category
   - **Title**: Order Total Share by Product Category for 2023

3. **Table**:
   - Columns: Product Name, Order Total
